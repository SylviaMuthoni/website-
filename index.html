WHO ARE WE?

At Umeme Sense solutions, we are on a mission to revolutionize energy management for industries. Umeme Sense is a smart&intelligent electricity meter designed to help industries monitor and optimize electricity consumption. By combining real-time tracking, automation, and AI-driven insights, our meter enables industries to reduce costs, improve efficiency, and enhance sustainability.

With features like detailed analytics, predictive diagnostics, and carbon emissions tracking, Umeme Sense helps industries detect inefficiencies, prevent equipment failures, and seamlessly integrate renewable energy. It gives industries greater control over their power usage, allowing them to make informed decisions that drive operational excellence and a greener future.

Our Mission

To empower industries with real-time, data-driven energy management solutions that enhance efficiency, reduce waste, and drive sustainability through our innovation and technology.

Our Vision

To become leading innovators in energy management, driving the global transition towards efficient, cost-effective, and renewable energy solutions for a greener and more sustainable world.

FEATURES
